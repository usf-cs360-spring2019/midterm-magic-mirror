Theme

Our high level objective is to use data relating to the San Francisco Fire Department to create visualizations to reveal trends or interesting statistics. The theme for our project is that the number of call types for a category relates to the reduction in the average response time for that call type. Essentially, the San Francisco Fire Department has increased their efficiency on the most common calls they receive. Additionally, we show the most common calls in the most well known neighborhoods of San Francisco. Navigate to our prototypes section to see interesting graphs and interesting findings!
